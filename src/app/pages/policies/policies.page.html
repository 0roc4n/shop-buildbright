Refund and Returns Policy

Overview

Our refund and return policy are only limited seven days (7) days from receipt of your purchased items/products. Beyond such thirty (30) days, a refund or exchange is no longer available.

Receipt or Proof of Purchase

Receipt or proof of purchase is required, mandatory and necessary. Returns should be addressed to our store and not to the manufacturer indicated in the item.

To be Eligible for a Full Refund/Return

To be eligible for a full refund/return within the seven (7) day period, the subject item/s must be unused and in the same condition that you received it, with the original packaging.

Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted for the following reasons:

  • Book with obvious signs of use
  • CD, DVD, VHS tape, software, video game, cassette tape, or vinyl record that has signs or indication of being opened
  • Item not in its original condition, damaged, or has missing parts, through fault, error, or mistake not attributable to us
  • Item/s returned beyond seven (7) days from receipt

Items Not Eligible for Refund/Return

We do not offer any refund/return for the following items:

  • Perishable goods such as food, flowers, newspapers or magazines
  • Intimate or sanitary goods, hazardous materials, or flammable liquids or gases
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Health and personal care items
  • Items on sale (Only regular priced items are covered)

Processing Refund/Return

Once the item subject of return/refund is received and inspected by our office, an email and/or text message will be sent to your registered email address and/or registered contact number, to notify you that we have received your returned item, subject for inspection and verification. Within 3-5 days thereof, we will send another notification on whether your return/refund is approved or denied.

Late or Missing Refunds

If the fault, error, or mistake in refund is attributable to our office, as determined by your bank, please contact us at cuatechgroup@gmail.com.

Item Exchange

Item replacement is only available for defective or damaged items, the fault, error, or mistake of which, being attributable to us.

If you need to exchange it for the same item, send us an email first at cuatechgroup@gmail.com and then send the item subject of exchange to the provided address.

Gifts

If the item purchased is classified as a “Gift,” it is the purchaser who will receive the gift credit for the value of the return, whether the return was made by the purchaser himself/herself or by the recipient of such gift.

Once the returned item is received, a “Gift Certificate” will be mailed to the registered email address of the purchaser.

Shipping Returns

Kindly mail your product to the provided address. Shipping Cost are not part of the refund. The Shipper shall shoulder the shipping cost for returning the item. If the Shipper did not pay the shipping cost and later on was granted a refund, the shipping cost for the return of the item shall be deducted from the refund granted. In case of returns of expensive items, the purchaser may consider using a trackable shipping service or purchasing a shipping insurance. We shall not be responsible for any damage/defect resulting from shipping of the item.
